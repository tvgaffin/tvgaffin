Resin

Resin art has a lot of similarities to my acrylic paintings but with so much more! It is more vibrant and has more depth than what can be achieved with acrylics. I can add textures to these paintings by incorporating add-ins like crystals and glitters. I also do works of encapsulation with resin, giving my art a 3-D effect. Resin has more fluidity that paints but lacks the array of colors than paints. However, I can mix acrylic paints and mica powders to achieve the desired color I am wanting. Resin is tedious to work with. There is a lot of mixing involved and it can be hazardous. Plus, there are other factors like work time and clean up. Once resin begins to set you are unable to make any changes. Though this is a harder medium to work with it helps with me mental by giving my mind something to dedicate my complete focus on and keeps my intrusive thoughts at bay.
